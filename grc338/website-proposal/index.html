Introduction

The Jasmine Dragon Tea Shop

The Jasmine Dragon Tea Shop sells high-quality loose leaf teas and teabag sets and teamaking supplies. We also brew fresh tea and offer pastries which you can enjoy in a relaxing and peaceful setting.

Target audience

The target audience includes tea enthusiasts, as well as newcomers who want to learn about teamaking.

Customers will use the website to browse the shop's tea selections and teamaking supplies which they can order in the store. The website will also feature a helpful guide for those who are inexperienced with teamaking and drinking. The website will also list the store's locations and contact information.

Additional Resources

How to Steep Loose Leaf Tea at Home

  • Bring water in a kettle to a rolling boil.
  • Place one teaspoon of loose leaves per cup of tea in a tea infuser such as our signature Dragon Infuser tool.
  • Pour the heated water over the tea infuser, making sure that the tea is completely covered.
  • Steep leaves for 3-5 minutes (for a stronger taste, use more tea leaves. Do not steep for a longer time).
  • Enjoy!
